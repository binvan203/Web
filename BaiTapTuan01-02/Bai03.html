One particular book which is recommended reading is The Street Lawyerby John Grisham. This book is about a lawyer who beagins re-evaluating his priorities in life when a bad incident occurs within his law firm. Consequently, he becomes acquainted with the inner city stressts, and realizes the harsh existence of the homeless, and vows to give them a chance in the courts. The Stress Lawyer is a great book. It is well written and interesting. Other book by John Grisham include The Film, The Pelican Brief, and The Client.
