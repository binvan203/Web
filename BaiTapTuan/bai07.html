A smile is a facial expression formed primarily by flexing the muscles at the sides of the mouth. It is believed it takes about 80 muscles to smile. Some smile include a contraction of the muscles at the cornet of the eyes, an action known as a "Dechenne smile". Smiles preformed without the eye contraction may be preceived as insincere.
